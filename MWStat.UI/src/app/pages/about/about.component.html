About Application

Graph

This app allows you to gather data on accounts which interacted with your profile by following or unfollowing.

After you gather data, current followers and followings will save to database, which allow us to determine who subscribed or unsubscribed in next data gathering.

Based on those data stamps we can draw graph, which display curve of followers/followings count to timeline.

You can interact with graph by specify timeline or clicking on graph points which allow us to specify timeline.

New followers and followings

After you selected specific timeline, you can see new and lost followers/followings.

You can subscribe or unsubscribe to displayed accounts.
